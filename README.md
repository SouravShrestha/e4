# ♟️ e4

> Ready for your next great move?

A tiny, no-fuss chess board that thinks right alongside you. It's your personal
coach: you play your side, it whispers a strong move whenever you want one — all
in your browser, no sign-ups, no nonsense.

Named after chess's most famous opening move. (Yes, the pun is on purpose.)

---

## ✨ What you get

- 💡 **Hints for your side** - a little arrow points you toward a strong move whenever you're stuck
- 🪄 **Follow any game** - enter your opponent's moves yourself and keep getting nudges for your replies
- 🧠 **A real engine in your pocket** - Stockfish runs right inside your browser
- 🎚️ **Five difficulty levels** - from "gentle nudge" to "okay, it's actually good now"
- ↩️ **Rewind anytime** - step back and forth through every move you've played
- 🎨 **Themes & dark mode** - make it feel like yours
- 🔊 **Satisfying click-clacks** - sounds for moves, captures, and checks
- 📱 **Phone or laptop** - it just fits

---

## 🎮 How to play

1. Pick your side — **White**, **Black**, or **Random** 🎲
2. Hit **Start**
3. On **your** turn, peek at the suggested move (or ignore it and play your own!)
4. On your **opponent's** turn, just play their move on the board — no spoilers there
5. Tap **⚙️ Settings** to flip sides, change the theme, set the difficulty, or mute the sounds

---

## 🎚️ How tough do you want it?

Five levels, easy to hard. **Level 1** is friendly and quick; **Level 5** actually
makes you work for it. The harder levels take a moment longer to answer — that
little pause is just the engine doing its homework. 🤔

---

## 🤓 The fine print

- Everything happens **in your browser** — no accounts, nothing uploaded, your games stay with you.
- The "brain" is **Stockfish**, one of the strongest chess engines on the planet, shrunk down to run on a web page.
